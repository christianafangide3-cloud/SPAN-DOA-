ANA 213: EMBRYOLOGY

The Chronological Biological Cascade

Stage 1: Foundation

  • [TRAP] Aneuploidies & Microdeletions
  • Spermatogenesis takes 74 Days
  • Oogenesis arrests at Dictyotene (Meiosis I)
  • Ovarian Cycle: GnRH → FSH/LH

Stage 2: Weeks 1 & 2

  • Day 1: Capacitation & Cortical Reaction
  • Days 2-5: Zygote → Morula → Blastocyst
  • Week 2 (Week of Twos): Implantation
  • Trophoblast → Cyto & Syncytio hCG
  • Embryoblast → Epiblast & Hypoblast

Stage 3: Gastrulation

  • Primitive Streak forms on Epiblast
  • FGF8 triggers migration
  • Trilaminar Disc: Ecto, Meso, Endoderm
  • Axes: PITX2 controls Left-Right sidedness

Stage 4: Organogenesis

  • Ectoderm: Neural Tube closes Day 25/28
  • Neural Crest 4th Germ Layer → Melanocytes
  • Mesoderm: Somites (SHH, WNT, NT-3)
  • Endoderm: Primitive Gut Tube formation

Stage 5: Systemic

  • Gut: Stomach rotates 90°, Midgut 270°
  • Pharyngeal: Arch 1 (Malleus), Pouch 3 (Thymus)
  • Cardiac: Tube loops RIGHT (PITX2)
  • Urogenital: SRY → Testes (Wolffian)

Stage 6: Fetal Clinics

  • [TRAP] Polyhydramnios vs Oligohydramnios
  • Twinning: Dizygotic is always DiDi
  • [TERATOLOGY] Thalidomide, FAS, TORCH
